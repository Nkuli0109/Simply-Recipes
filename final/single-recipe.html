Banana Pancakes

Fluffy Banana Pancakes are easy to make, naturally sweet & make the best freezer-friendly pancakes. There's nothing like a delicious, fluffy stack of pancakes in the morning and these Banana Pancakes are some of the best around! They are easy to make and taste amazing, with a little bit of Cinnamon and Banana flavor. Not only does the Banana add flavor, but it also adds mositure and natural sweetness. These Pancakes are sure to be a hit with the whole family! If you love homemade Pancakes then this recipe is for you! Feel free to garnish with chopped Berries and some Syrup or even Honey for extra flavor & sweetness!

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Pork breakfast Onions Soup

instructions

step 1

Mash 2 overripe Bananas till smooth, add a cup of milk and 2 eggs. Whisk the wet ingredients together and set aside.

step 2

In a large bowl put together 100g all purpose flour, a pinch of salt, a cup of sugar and 1 tbsp of Cinnamon (optiona) Mix together all dry ingredients.

step 3

Take wet ingredients and pour on the dry ingredients, mix well till batter is smooth. On a pan, heat up 1 tbsp of cooking oil and pour in the batter using a scoop. Give each side 3 mins to cook before removing them from the pan.

ingredients

100g all purpose flour

2 Large eggs

1 cup milk

2 Large overripe Bananas

tools

Whisk

Large Pan

Measuring Spoons

Large mixing Bowl
